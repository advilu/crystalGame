Crystal Math Guessing Game

Welcome! Here, if you want to succeed, you will need both luck and the power of mathematics. May the crystals be forever with you!

The game has automatically begun. To play, click a crystal, as many crystals as you like, as many times as you like. Each crystal has chosen a numerical value that it will add to your Total when you click it. But beware! You only become the champion of the game if you match your Total points value to the Target value.

When you win or lose, press the reset button to play again. And, remember--your number of wins and losses will be recorded for posterity, so play wisely.
